QUALITY POLICY

NNPC Medical Services Limited(NMSL) Mosimi Committed to providing sustainable, consistent, exceptional, quality, excellent, accessible and safe world class healthcare services, anchored on a value-based patient centered care and safety for patient delight.

NMSL Mosimi is committed to satisfying applicable requirements and continually improving the ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System to deliver exceptional health care services and patient safety.

To this end, measurable quality objectives have been set and communicated to relevant stakeholders as required in order to ensure the continous delivery of quality services.

QMS Objectives

1. To promote and ensure continous delivery of efficient world class quality health care services to our patients.

2. To achieve 95% customer satisfaction consistently.

3. To ensure strict adherence to clinical protoclos and achieve improved clinical outcomes.

4. Ensure 99% drug availability on a monthly basis.

5. Achieve 100% compliance to all applicable requirement.

6. To ensure all comapny personnel are fully competent to carry out their assigned task.

7. To review the Quality Management System annually to ensure its continous sustianability, effectivess and adequacy.
